Nothing is permanent and change is the only constant- is one of the many things that you may have learnt in your professional and personal journey. It may have reinforced time and again especially when unprecedented COVID hit us and everything changed overnight. To cite an instance of this learning, you would have certainly witnessed the everchanging trends and patterns since the inception of information age. Currently, electric vehicles, artificial intelligence, 5G technology and genomics are expected to be some of the sunrise sectors of growth. In few years, it could be something else just like how NFT has been doing rounds in the last few weeks. We need to be prepared, bold, courageous, and welcome these new developments and look at it with a perspective of “anything and everything can be transformed into an opportunity” by changing our mental models and with agility and speed.

PRINCIPLES IN ACTION

Williams Sonoma is already the number one non- pureplay ecommerce retailer in home furnishings and a top 25 e-commerce retailer in the US across all industries, that sells kitchen-wares and home furnishings. The company had spent its time, money, and efforts towards omnichannel transformation over the years; it paid off during the COVID crisis and it will continue to pay well even in the post pandemic world.

One of the key reasons Williams Sonoma outperformed was because of their e-commerce platform which was able to serve the need of the customers well. The shift of the home industry online uniquely positioned them in the market. They acquired customers at record rates all year and the customer retention metrics continue to improve among new customers.

Williams – Sonoma’s digital transformation journey is a case in point which illustrates few of the principles that we are talking about in our book: challenge your mental models and always stay ahead, reimagine and reinvent your approach to customers and stakeholders, speed up digital transformation and design for personalization at scale, to name a few. If you want to learn more about each of these principles, please feel free to download the introduction of our book from here.
